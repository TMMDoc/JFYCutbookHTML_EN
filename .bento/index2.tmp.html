Thank you for choosing JFY Cut.

JFY Cut is an Integrated Sheet-metal CAM application. Using JFY Cut, you can convert your sheet metal designs into CAM data.

JFY Cut leverages a unified environment, from which one can program all the different technology machines within the sheet metal shop floor.

JFY Cut can be used integrated within JFY Plus system seamlessly for doing advanced resource planning, shop-floor management and large-volume batch processing.

An innovative and futuristic, yet easy to learn platform, for the classic sheet metal factory setup, providing optimal performance deliverance of productivity.
